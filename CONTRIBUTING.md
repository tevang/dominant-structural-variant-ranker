# Contributing

## Scientific-Claim Discipline

Do not add new chemistry claims unless they are documented in the code, tests, and docs. If a method only provides approximate ranking, label it approximate.

For this project, pH/protomer generation, tautomer/stereoisomer enumeration, conformer ranking, and population estimates must keep their stated scientific scope. In particular, do not imply rigorous pH-dependent populations across protonation states unless an explicit micro-pKa or proton chemical-potential correction method is implemented, tested, and documented.
